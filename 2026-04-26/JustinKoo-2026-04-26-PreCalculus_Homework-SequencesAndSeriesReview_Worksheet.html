📐 Formula Sheet
Type$n$th termSum
Arithmetic$t_n = t_1 + (n-1)\, d$$S_n = \dfrac{n}{2}(t_1 + t_n) = \dfrac{n}{2}\big(2 t_1 + (n-1) d\big)$
Geometric$t_n = t_1 \cdot r^{n-1}$$S_n = t_1 \cdot \dfrac{r^n - 1}{r - 1} \;\; (r \neq 1)$
Infinite Geometric—$S_\infty = \dfrac{t_1}{1 - r} \;\; (\,|r| < 1\,)$
🧪 EXAMPLE 1Arithmetic — finding a term
Given the arithmetic sequence with $t_1 = 7$ and $t_2 = 21$, find $t_{20}$.
Step 1. Common difference: $d = t_2 - t_1 = 21 - 7 = 14$.
Step 2. Apply $t_n = t_1 + (n-1)\, d$:
$t_{20} = 7 + (20 - 1)(14) = 7 + 19 \times 14 = 7 + 266 = 273$
$\boxed{\; t_{20} = 273 \;}$
🧪 EXAMPLE 2Geometric — finding a term
Given the geometric sequence with $t_1 = 3$ and $t_2 = 6$, find $t_{10}$.
Step 1. Common ratio: $r = \dfrac{t_2}{t_1} = \dfrac{6}{3} = 2$.
Step 2. Apply $t_n = t_1 \cdot r^{n-1}$:
$t_{10} = 3 \cdot 2^{10 - 1} = 3 \cdot 2^9 = 3 \cdot 512 = 1536$
$\boxed{\; t_{10} = 1536 \;}$
